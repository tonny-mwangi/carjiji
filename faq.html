Frequently Asked Questions

How can I rent a car from Car Jiji?

To rent a car from Jiji Car Rental, you can either visit our website and book online or contact our customer service team to assist you with the booking process.

What is included in the rental price?

The rental price typically includes basic insurance coverage and unlimited mileage. Additional services such as GPS navigation, child seats, and insurance upgrades may be available for an extra fee.

Are the cars new or used?

We offer both brand new and certified pre-owned vehicles to cater to various customer preferences.

Can I choose the specific car model I want to rent?

Yes, you can select the car model you prefer when booking online. However, availability may vary depending on factors such as location and booking date.

What are the requirements for renting a car?

Generally, you must be at least 21 years old, have a valid driver's license, and provide a valid form of identification such as a passport or ID card. Additional requirements may vary depending on the specific car and rental location.

What sets Car Jiji apart from other car rentals companies?

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Our extensive network, expertise, and personalized service ensure a smooth and enjoyable car-renting experience.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy allows for free cancellation up to 48 hours before the scheduled pick-up time. Cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee.
