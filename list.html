Steps to List Your Car at Car-Jiji

Step 1

Get in touch with us through our website or customer desk in our offices regarding your request. Our represetatives will confirm the availability of the service and will give you a feedback. You will be required provide your contact details.

Step 2

Once our office has verified your request and decided to go ahead with the processing, Car Jiji will send a staff to pay you a visit and inspect the car you're looking to list with us. A verification process will take place for a max of 3 days.

Step 3

After the verification process is over, your car will be assigned a unique ID and included in our databases as a new asset for Car Jiji. Terms will be discussed and there you go, you've listed your car with the top car rental company in Kenya.
