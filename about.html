What We Offer

Car Rentals

We hire out cars to individuals looking to self drive for weekends, road trips or any other personal use.

Airport Pick-ups

We offer affordable yet premium quality airport pick-ups services for or clients landing from abroad at all Kenyan airports.

Events Convoys

For events such as weddings, graduations, corporate events, we provide high quality convoys at a very fair pricing

Car Sales

Car Jiji offers our clients a chance to own their dream cars. We offer used vehicles and directly imported new cars.

VIP Convoys

We have a wide range of brand new cars available for higher for exclusive and premium VIP convoys. We additionally provide competent drivers for such convoys.

Car Financing

In your efforts to own a car, Car Jiji is more than willing to finance your dreams come true with a very affordable plan and interest rates. Reach out to us for any enquiry.
